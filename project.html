Our Vision

GoldChain Investment is designed to provide a structured digital participation experience with secure access, transparent user tracking, and controlled verification processes. We focus on discipline, clarity, and trust.

How It Works

  • Create an account (Sign Up).
  • Select a plan that matches your goal.
  • Submit your deposit request.
  • Admin verifies the payment.
  • Your dashboard updates balance & status.
  • Withdrawals are processed through a controlled approval flow.

Two-Level Referral System

Each user has a personal referral code. When someone signs up using your code and completes a verified deposit, you earn referral commission. The system also supports 2-level structure (direct + indirect).

  • Level 1: Commission from your direct referral after verified deposit.
  • Level 2: Commission from your referral’s referral after verified deposit.

*Rates and thresholds can be configured by admin policy.

Security & Transparency

  • Secure login system (Firebase Authentication).
  • Verified deposits before balance update.
  • Controlled withdrawal approvals.
  • Recorded histories (payments, withdrawals, referral records).
  • Admin access control and audit logs.
